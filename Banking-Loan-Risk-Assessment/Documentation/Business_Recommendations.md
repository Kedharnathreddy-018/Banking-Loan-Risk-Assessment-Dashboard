# Business Recommendations

1. Tighten approval rules for applicants with low credit scores and high debt-to-income ratios.
2. Launch branch-level monitoring for default spikes and payment delinquency.
3. Use payment compliance trends as an early-warning signal for collections teams.
4. Segment lending campaigns by occupation, income band, and state-level approval behavior.
5. Combine predictive risk scores with rule-based policies instead of using model outputs alone.

## Risk mitigation strategy

- Introduce risk-based pricing for moderate and high-risk segments.
- Route high-risk applications for manual review before approval.
- Trigger proactive reminders for customers with falling compliance scores.
- Review branch underwriting patterns where approval rates are high but default rates are also high.
